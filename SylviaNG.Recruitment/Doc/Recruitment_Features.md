| 1   | Candidate Profile & CV Management       | Provides a unified profile for both external and internal candidates. An internal job board gives employees a job search interface to search, browse, and apply for open positions.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                  | SylviaHCM Portion |

Includes AI-powered resume screening, keyword parsing, candidate tagging, talent pool creation, and candidate ranking against customizable criteria. Supports admin-applied applications on a candidate's behalf, duplicate detection (agency vs. direct applicant), and real-time status tracking throughout the recruitment pipeline.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                       |                   |

Also supports seat plan generation, bulk admit card distribution (SMS/email), and Exam Hall Management to assign and manage physical or virtual exam venues, seating arrangements, invigilators, and hall capacities for written and aptitude tests.                                               |                   |

Supports multiple interview sessions per job, bulk interview scheduling, configuration of interview assessments, and export of evaluation data for review, along with venue scheduling to assign and manage physical interview locations, room bookings, and venue details for each session.                                                                                              |                   |
